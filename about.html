About Institute Technical Council (ITC)

The Institute Technical Council (ITC) at IIT Bombay is committed to fostering a strong technical culture on campus. It serves as the central body that promotes innovation, hands-on learning, and technical excellence among students.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, tools, and platforms needed to explore, create, and innovate in diverse technical fields.

Our Goals

  • Encourage technical skill development through competitions, workshops, and mentorship programs.
  • Promote interdisciplinary collaboration among technical clubs and student bodies.
  • Support and represent student-led technical initiatives at the institute and beyond.
