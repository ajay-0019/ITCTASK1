Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the Institute Technical Council (ITC)?

The ITC is the central body at IIT Bombay that promotes technical culture and innovation among students.

How can I get involved with ITC?

You can join various technical clubs, participate in events, or volunteer for committees organized by ITC.

What events does ITC organize?

ITC organizes a variety of events including workshops, hackathons, and technical competitions.
